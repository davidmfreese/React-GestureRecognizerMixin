Example Details

This example has three seperate gesture recognizers:

  • Pan Gesture - Click/Tap and drag the yellow square around
  • Tap Gesture (1 tap) - Tap once and only once to toggle the squares size from little to normal.
  • Tap Gesture (4 taps) - Tap four time (only four times) and toggle the size from large to normal.

Tap Gesture (1 tap) requires Tap Gesture (4 taps) to fail.
